Enterprise Visual AI Startup Support

Industry: AI Project: Enterprise Visual AI Startup Support Role: Consultant / Growth & Visibility Advisor

Synopsis

Supported an early-stage visual AI startup building enterprise solutions for oil and gas clients. The startup faced funding challenges from local government programs and venture capitalists who considered the business too early-stage for enterprise adoption.

The Challenge

  • Government funding rules restricted allocation for hiring staff, limiting operational capacity.
  • Venture capitalists viewed the company as too early-stage for enterprise-scale solutions.
  • Needed increased visibility to attract investors and strategic partners.

The Solution

  • Assisted with investor outreach, pitching to VCs and angel investors to broaden funding opportunities.
  • Enhanced the startup’s social media presence to increase visibility and credibility.
  • Secured media coverage including a BFM interview to highlight the startup’s technology and potential.

The Results

  • Raised awareness of the startup within local and regional investor communities.
  • Boosted credibility and visibility through targeted media exposure.
  • Although the startup eventually shut down, the engagement improved outreach, investor engagement, and brand presence during its operation.
