Sleep Tracking Eye Patch Startup Consultation

Consumer Hardware / Wearables Product Design & Data Strategy Consultant / Product & UX Advisor

Synopsis

Assisted a consumer hardware startup developing an eye patch wearable to monitor sleep patterns. The goal was to optimize product design, data collection, and user experience for accurate sleep tracking.

The Challenge

  • Limited experience in wearable hardware and sleep science integration.
  • Need to balance comfort, sensor accuracy, and battery life.
  • Required a seamless user experience and reliable data analytics for sleep insights.

The Solution

  • Advised on sensor placement, hardware design, and ergonomics for nightly wear.
  • Designed a data collection and analytics framework for accurate sleep tracking.
  • Guided prototyping and iterative testing to refine both comfort and functionality.
  • Provided recommendations for mobile app integration to visualize user sleep patterns.

The Results

  • Improved sleep tracking accuracy through iterative design and testing.
  • Enhanced user comfort and adoption potential.
  • Established a roadmap for scalable production and future feature expansion.
