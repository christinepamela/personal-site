Intermittent Failures in Data Acquisition Unit

Asian NDK faced recurring “OVLD” errors in a Data Acquisition Unit that standard service checks classified as “No Trouble Found.” Through controlled onsite experiments, I isolated the issue to the DAQ mainframe’s communication fault, ruling out external modules and fixtures.

Identified the faulty DAQ mainframe as the true root cause, preventing further NTF cycles and restoring production reliability.
