Scaling Mestrae: From Local Startup to Global Retail Presence

Fashion / Retail Retail Expansion / Global Distribution Strategy Founder & Strategist

Synopsis

Mestrae, my own fashion-tech startup specializing in interchangeable heels, expanded from a local brand to international markets. The project focused on establishing a global retail presence, forming strategic distribution partnerships, and navigating operational challenges in multiple regions.

The Challenge

  • Local government dishonored contractual funding, creating financial and operational constraints.
  • Lack of established distribution channels in international markets.
  • Retail expansion required balancing consignment and outright purchase models while maintaining brand exclusivity.
  • Need to build brand recognition and customer trust from scratch in foreign markets.

The Solution

  • Expanded into international markets via a Spanish distributor and boutique/mall outlets across key cities.
  • Implemented both consignment and direct purchase retail models to optimize cash flow and market reach.
  • Developed marketing and operational strategies to establish brand presence and customer loyalty in diverse markets.
  • Leveraged own product development and internal resources to overcome local funding setbacks.

The Results

  • Successfully launched retail outlets in Kuala Lumpur, California, New York, and London.
