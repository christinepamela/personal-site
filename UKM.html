Guest Lecture on Electronics Testing for Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM)

Education / Electronics Industry knowledge on Electronics Testing Guest Lecturer

Synopsis

Delivered a hands-on, industry-aligned guest lecture on electronics testing techniques, sharing real-world insights and best practices with undergraduate and postgraduate engineering students.

The Challenge

Engineering students often have strong theoretical knowledge but limited exposure to practical electronics testing challenges faced in industry. Bridging the gap between theory and practice was essential to prepare students for careers in electronics and manufacturing.

The Solution

  • Designed a lecture combining theory, case studies, and practical demonstrations.
  • Introduced industry-standard tools such as digital multimeters (DMMs), oscilloscopes, and EMC pre-compliance setups.
  • Shared real-world problem-solving examples from prior engineering consulting projects.
  • Engaged students through Q&A sessions, troubleshooting exercises, and mini hands-on experiments.

The Results

  • Enhanced students’ understanding of practical electronics testing.
  • Provided exposure to real-world challenges and industry workflows.
  • Strengthened UKM’s collaboration with industry professionals.
