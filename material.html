Optimizing Sustainable Manufacturing for Plant-Based Fibre Brand

Sustainable Textiles / Supply Chain Process and Supply Chain Optimization Consultant

Synopsis

Consulted with a consumer goods brand specializing in plant-based fibre textiles blended with cotton. The engagement focused on improving manufacturing efficiency, reducing waste, and scaling sustainable practices while maintaining product quality.

The Challenge

  • Manufacturing processes for plant-based fibres were new and required fine-tuning to maintain quality.
  • Blending with cotton introduced variability affecting yield and consistency.
  • Supply chain lacked integrated tracking for sustainable materials, leading to inefficiencies and higher costs.
  • Brand needed to scale production without compromising sustainability commitments.

The Solution

  • Conducted process audits and implemented optimization strategies to reduce production time and material waste.
  • Introduced quality control protocols specifically adapted to plant-based fibre blends.
  • Streamlined supply chain with tracking systems for sustainable sourcing and inventory management.
  • Advised on scalable production practices to maintain eco-friendly standards while increasing output.
