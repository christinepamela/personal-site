Establishing a Full-Scale Calibration Laboratory

Electronics / Calibration & Quality Assurance Lab Setup / Compliance Readiness Technical Manager / Lead Consultant

Synopsis

Built a complete calibration laboratory from the ground up, encompassing infrastructure, test equipment procurement, process design, staffing, and compliance readiness.

The Challenge

The client required a fully operational calibration laboratory to support internal testing and certification needs. This included creating processes aligned with international audit standards, procuring equipment, setting up test stations, and establishing a qualified technical team. The challenge was not only technical setup but also ensuring the lab could scale while meeting compliance and audit expectations.

The Solution

As technical manager and lead consultant, I designed and executed the entire laboratory build-out. This included:

  • Identifying and procuring the necessary test equipment.
  • Designing the calibration process and workflows to align with industry and audit standards.
  • Establishing five test stations to handle initial low-frequency testing.
  • Developing standard operating procedures (SOPs), documentation, and compliance protocols.
  • Structuring the organization with org charts, defining roles, and recruiting the right technical talent.

The Results

  • Successful establishment of a fully functional calibration lab with five operational test stations.
  • Streamlined audit-ready processes ensuring long-term compliance and certification readiness.
  • A skilled team and organizational structure in place to support ongoing calibration and expansion into higher frequency domains.
  • Significantly reduced dependency on external calibration services, improving turnaround time and cost efficiency.
