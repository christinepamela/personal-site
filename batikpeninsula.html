Synopsis

The Challenge

  • Positioning traditional crafts in modern international markets without diluting cultural authenticity.
  • Navigating export logistics, regulations, and international retail partnerships.
  • Enhancing brand appeal to global consumers while maintaining artisan identity.

The Solution

  • Developed branding and marketing strategies tailored for international markets.
  • Advised on product packaging and storytelling to resonate with global audiences.
  • Created a roadmap for distribution partnerships, e-commerce platforms, and trade shows.
  • Provided guidance on operational scaling, quality control, and export compliance.
