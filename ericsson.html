Consulting on 5G Technology Implementation for Ericsson Malaysia (early research)

Telecommunications / 5G Infrastructure Technology Strategy & Implementation Consulting Consultant / Strategic Advisor

Synopsis

Partnered with Ericsson Malaysia to advise on strategic 5G technology deployment, harmonizing their global advancements with regional goals. The project aligned technical innovation including 5G Advanced and AI-driven network operations with local manufacturing, infrastructure, and enterprise digitalization strategies.

The Challenge

Ericsson, a global leader in telecommunications, had embarked on key initiatives in Malaysia: advancing its 5G infrastructure, initiating local manufacturing of 5G hardware, and supporting enterprise transformation across industries. The challenge was to integrate these ambitions into practical roadmap strategies early on while matching Malaysia’s priorities around digital leadership, economic impact, and technological sustainability.

The Solution

I delivered consulting support that spanned several strategic fronts:

  • Aligning Technical Strategy with Local Ecosystem – helped parallel Ericsson’s global launch of 5G Advanced and AI-driven network operations with Malaysia’s local manufacturing capabilities, notably their new 5G radio production facility in Penang.
  • Bridging Manufacturing and Field Deployment – supported early design of workflows and test protocols to ensure quality and reliability between facility and live deployment on Malaysia’s network.
  • Promoting Enterprise 5G Adoption – positioned Ericsson’s smart-industry and energy-efficient 5G offerings to enable use cases in O&G, smart manufacturing, and IoT.

Technologies Used

  • 5G Advanced features including AI intent-based network operations.
  • Massive MIMO radio equipment produced locally.
  • Enterprise-grade 5G application frameworks for O&G, smart manufacturing, and IoT.
  • Strategic planning and test protocol design to align manufacturing with deployment.

The Results

  • Supported seamless integration between local manufacturing and national 5G rollouts, ensuring both quality and scalability.
  • Positioned Ericsson as a key enabler of Malaysia's digital enterprise ecosystem, particularly in sectors like oil & gas and smart industry.
  • Demonstrated how production excellence (via local manufacturing) and network innovations (advanced 5G) can synergize to build Malaysia’s digital leadership.
