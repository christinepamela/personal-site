Banking Market Research & Customer Service Assessment

Banking & Financial Services / Customer Experience Market Research & Customer Service Assessment Consultant / Customer Experience Analyst

Synopsis

Conducted market research and customer service evaluation for a banking client to understand customer needs, satisfaction levels, and service quality across multiple branches and digital touchpoints.

The Challenge

  • Assessing customer satisfaction and service quality across multiple channels (branches, call centers, and digital platforms).
  • Identifying gaps in customer experience and areas for improvement.
  • Collecting reliable feedback while minimizing disruption to daily operations.

The Solution

  • Designed and administered surveys, interviews, and mystery shopper exercises.
  • Analyzed service processes, wait times, and responsiveness across touchpoints.
  • Synthesized insights into actionable recommendations for service enhancement.

The Results

  • Delivered a detailed report on customer satisfaction, service gaps, and operational bottlenecks.
  • Provided actionable recommendations for staff training, process improvement, and digital service optimization.
  • Enabled the bank to implement targeted initiatives to enhance customer experience and loyalty.
