Revenue Optimization and Retail Distribution Expansion for Global Consumer Goods Brand

Consumer Goods / Retail Revenue Optimization / Supply Chain Consultant / Pricing & Supply Chain Advisor

Synopsis

Provided end-to-end consulting for a multinational consumer goods company, starting from scratch to establish retail and distribution initiatives, while optimizing pricing and supply chain operations. The engagement focused on harmonizing regional pricing, reducing operational costs, and improving net margins without compromising premium brand positioning.

The Challenge

  • Rising supply chain costs were eroding profitability across multiple regions.
  • Inconsistent regional pricing created market inefficiencies and impacted brand perception.
  • Fragmented supplier contracts and lack of centralized tracking limited operational agility.
  • Retail and distribution infrastructure were minimal, requiring foundational setup from scratch.

The Solution

  • Designed a global pricing harmonization framework to align regional prices with market positioning.
  • Renegotiated supplier contracts to reduce cost base and improve terms.
  • Introduced a centralized dashboard for real-time tracking of pricing and supply chain performance.
  • Built retail and distribution networks from the ground up, including channel mapping, partner selection, and operational guidelines.

The Results

  • Increased net margins by 6% across APAC and EMEA markets.
  • Achieved $4.2M in annualized savings.
  • Strengthened premium brand positioning through consistent regional pricing.
  • Established a functional retail and distribution network, enabling scalable market expansion.
  • Improved visibility, control, and agility in pricing and supply chain operations.
