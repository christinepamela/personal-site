Pam's Corner

Advancing High-Precision Testing for Xyratex and Seagate

Electronics Testing High-Precision Measurement & Validation Consultant / Technical Trainer

Synopsis

Xyratex (later acquired by Seagate) specialized in storage systems and HDD/SSD testing solutions. To support their product reliability goals, we provided advanced measurement expertise using nanometer-range digital multimeters (DMM) and impedance testing. The engagement enhanced accuracy in component validation, accelerated troubleshooting, and reinforced confidence in the performance of mission-critical storage technologies.

The Challenge

Both Xyratex and Seagate faced increasing demands for high-density storage reliability. Precision in measurement at the nanometer scale was critical for evaluating signal integrity and minimizing data errors. Existing workflows struggled with achieving repeatable accuracy under tight tolerances, particularly when testing impedance across complex circuits.

The Solution

  • Collaborated closely with engineering teams to deploy nanometer-range DMMs for ultra-fine resolution measurement.
  • Optimized impedance-related test setups to identify parasitic effects and reduce variability.
  • Provided practical training to ensure engineers could replicate high-accuracy measurement consistently in both R&D and production environments.

The Results

  • Improved measurement accuracy and consistency for HDD/SSD validation.
  • Faster identification of impedance-related issues in product design.
  • Enhanced engineer skill sets for advanced test execution.
  • Greater reliability and confidence in product performance for global storage markets.
