Synopsis

Conducted in-depth market research for a pharmaceutical company by engaging directly with doctors across hospitals and clinics to inform product strategy and positioning.

The Challenge

  • Needed accurate, first-hand insights from medical professionals to guide product strategy.
  • Doctors were busy and difficult to access, making data collection challenging.
  • Ensuring reliable, unbiased feedback across multiple regions.

The Solution

  • Developed a structured interview and survey framework for hospital and clinic visits.
  • Scheduled and conducted one-on-one discussions with doctors to gather qualitative and quantitative insights.
  • Analyzed responses to identify trends, unmet needs, and product positioning opportunities.

The Results

  • Delivered actionable insights on doctor preferences, prescribing habits, and regional market differences.
  • Provided recommendations for product positioning and marketing strategies.
  • Strengthened client understanding of key regional healthcare dynamics.
