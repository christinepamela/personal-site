Retail & Distributorship Strategy for Australian Sports Watch Brand

Fashion & Lifestyle / Retail Retail Expansion / Distributorship Business Consultant

Synopsis

Supported an Australian sports watch brand in expanding its retail footprint and managing distributorship channels in Southeast Asia.

The Challenge

  • Brand was relatively unknown in the target market.
  • Needed to establish effective distribution networks and retail presence.
  • Required alignment of pricing, promotions, and channel strategy to maximize reach and profitability.

The Solution

  • Designed a distributorship and retail expansion strategy tailored to local market dynamics.
  • Identified key retail partners and optimized distribution channels for efficiency and visibility.
  • Developed pricing, promotional, and positioning recommendations to support brand growth.
  • Leveraged retail and distributorship management frameworks, market analysis, consumer segmentation, and channel performance dashboards.

The Results

  • Expanded retail presence in strategic outlets across Southeast Asia.
  • Strengthened brand positioning and visibility in the sports watch category.
  • Optimized distribution networks leading to improved operational efficiency and sales growth.
