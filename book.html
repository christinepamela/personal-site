Innovation Underdogs: The Corporate Rebel Playbook

by Christine Pamela Chandrakasan
Entrepreneur • Strategist • Systems Builder

Book Overview

What happens when a breakthrough idea lands in a company that was never designed to change?

Innovation Underdogs is a strategic guide for building lasting innovation inside large, complex, or traditional organizations. With real-world case studies, visual frameworks, and grounded storytelling, it charts a path from resistance to reinvention—without breaking what already works.

At its core is the Theta Framework, a visual model that maps six types of innovation—Core, Adjacent, Architectural, Disruptive, Transformational, and Frontier—across three corporate zones: Core, Edge, and Beyond.

Why This Book, Why Now

Since 2000, over half of the Fortune 500 has vanished. McKinsey predicts the average S&P 500 lifespan will shrink to 12 years by 2027. While many companies claim to innovate, few rewire their identity when technology demands it.

This book fills the gap between startup idealism and corporate reality—offering a system that makes breakthrough innovation stick.
