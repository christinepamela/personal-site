Market Research on Broadcast Media Penetration

Media & Communications / Market Research Broadcast Media Penetration Study Consultant / Market Research Analyst

Synopsis

Conducted in-depth market research to assess broadcast media reach, audience preferences, and consumption patterns across multiple regions in Malaysia.

The Challenge

  • Needed a clear understanding of regional broadcast coverage, audience engagement, and media consumption habits.
  • Data collection had to cover urban and rural areas with diverse demographics.
  • Ensuring consistent and reliable data from various sources, including households and institutions.

The Solution

  • Designed surveys and interviews targeting viewers, households, and local institutions.
  • Collected data via field visits, phone interviews, and secondary research.
  • Analyzed results to identify coverage gaps, content preferences, and opportunities for audience growth.
  • Used survey and analytics platforms, geographic mapping tools, and statistical analysis software for audience insights.

The Results

  • Produced a comprehensive report on broadcast penetration, audience demographics, and content preferences.
  • Provided actionable recommendations for programming, coverage expansion, and targeted campaigns.
  • Enabled the client to optimize outreach and plan for strategic content investments.
