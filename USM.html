Guest Lecture and Test Equipment Consultation for Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM)

Education / Electronics Industry Consultation / Test Equipment Consultation Guest Lecturer / Consultant

Synopsis

Delivered an interactive guest lecture and provided consultancy on electronics test equipment, bridging academic knowledge with practical industry applications for engineering students and faculty.

The Challenge

Students and faculty had theoretical understanding of electronics testing but limited exposure to modern test equipment and real-world measurement challenges. USM sought to enhance hands-on capabilities and align teaching with industry practices.

The Solution

  • Conducted a lecture series with practical demonstrations of test equipment such as digital multimeters, oscilloscopes, and signal analyzers.
  • Offered consultancy on selection, calibration, and effective use of test instruments.
  • Shared real-life case studies from engineering projects to illustrate common challenges and solutions.

The Results

  • Improved students’ hands-on skills with modern testing instruments.
  • Strengthened faculty knowledge and confidence in teaching practical electronics testing.
  • Enhanced USM’s collaboration with industry professionals.
