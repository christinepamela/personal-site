Debugging and Strategic Test Upgrade for Photonics Production at QEOSS

Photonics / Electronics Test System Debug & Upgrade Strategy Consultant

Synopsis

Quantum Electro Opto Systems (QEOSS), a photonics systems integrator, faced challenges with a refurbished Lightwave Test Set used in production. The unit exhibited failures in optical signal paths, creating uncertainty in test reliability. I was engaged to troubleshoot the issue, provide a root-cause analysis, and advise on long-term production testing strategies.

The Challenge

  • A critical Lightwave Test Set was producing noisy or unusable optical signals while RF outputs seemed normal.
  • As QEOSS was preparing to scale photonics production, they needed confidence in their testing strategy.

The Solution

  • Conducted a step-by-step troubleshooting methodology across different signal paths (RF-in to RF-out, RF-in to optical-out, optical loops).
  • Identified that the optical path was the consistent point of failure, likely due to internal optical-electrical conversion faults.
  • Recommended two parallel actions:
    • Engage optical experts for deeper analysis to minimize downtime.
    • Request a demo of the modern Lightwave Component Analyzer for cross-comparison, while also introducing its benefits in terms of ease of use, accuracy, and long-term serviceability.

Technologies Used

  • Lightwave Test Set (legacy, refurbished)
  • Oscilloscope for RF observation
  • Network Analyzer (RF signal source at 500 MHz, 0 dBm)
  • Proposed as modern replacement: Lightwave Component Analyzer for photonic component analysis

The Results

  • Isolated the issue to the optical wave portion of the refurbished test set, reducing wasted time in other subsystems.
  • Helped QEOSS management see the risk of relying on unsupported/refurbished photonic test sets in production.
  • Developed a long-term strategic investment for scalable and reliable photonics testing.
  • Established trust by not only fixing the immediate technical issue but also advising on broader production and testing strategy.
