Reviving NXP’s Noise Figure Measurement System

Semiconductors / Electronics Test System Integration / Automation Consultant / Project Lead

Synopsis

A three-month, team of 3 project to modernize NXP’s QA test lab by scrapping its outdated noise figure test system and replacing it with a state-of-the-art, automated measurement solution.

The Challenge

NXP’s QA test lab relied on an old, legacy noise figure measurement setup that had become unreliable. The system suffered from calibration drift, obsolete instruments, long test cycles, and poor repeatability, limiting the team’s ability to validate new semiconductor designs with confidence.

The Solution

We scrapped the legacy system and implemented a modernized setup including a new noise figure analyzer, a low-noise calibrated source, and modular RF switching. Newly developed and deployed automation software replaced manual procedures, and traceable calibration was built in to ensure accuracy and repeatability.

The Results

  • Test cycle time reduced by 40%
  • Higher accuracy and repeatability in NF measurements
  • Enabled validation of next-generation RF devices
  • Freed engineers from troubleshooting legacy equipment to focus on design and QA validation
