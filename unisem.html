High-Frequency Product Demonstration for Unisem Engineering Teams

Semiconductor Corporate Training Trainer / Consultant

Synopsis

Delivered a one-day, hands-on demonstration of high-frequency test and measurement equipment to engineers, managers, and technicians at Unisem’s Ipoh facility. The session addressed real-world challenges they faced in mixed-signal and RF testing within semiconductor assembly and test operations.

The Challenge

Unisem, a global semiconductor assembly and test service provider, operates a full turnkey facility in Ipoh offering services ranging from wafer bumping to RF and mixed-signal final test. Their engineering staff spanned roles from test development to failure analysis and lab management. They needed concise, yet deep, orientation to the latest measurement tools and techniques, especially those addressing challenges in accuracy, repeatability, and complexity at high frequencies.

The Solution

  • Conducted a structured, interactive, one-day demo alongside expert consultants to showcase product capabilities and solve engineers’ challenges on the spot.
  • Technical overview of RF/mixed-signal measurement tools suited for semiconductor testing.
  • Live demonstrations tailored to Unisem’s process needs, e.g., accurate impedance, gain flatness, and intermodulation measurements.
  • Q&A and troubleshooting segments addressing real issues engineers encountered, showing how equipment could resolve them.

The Results

  • Engineers and managers gained hands-on familiarity with high-frequency measurement workflows tailored for assembly/test environments.
  • Delivered practical, immediate insights. Many real issues were simulated and resolved using the demo tools.
  • Built stronger alignment between Unisem’s test engineering team and instrument capabilities, facilitating faster resolution of future test bottlenecks.
  • Reinforced Unisem Ipoh’s reputation for technical excellence and continuous capability upgrading.
