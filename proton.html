Automating Keyless Production Testing for an Automotive Manufacturer

Automotive Automated Test System Consultant / Systems Integrator

Synopsis

Led a multi-month deployment of a pioneering automated measurement system for in-car electronic components, specifically keyless entry modules, for a major automaker. The project streamlined test accuracy and throughput in a traditionally slow-moving, protectionist production environment.

The Challenge

  • The automaker needed a keyless entry test suite that was both reliable and efficient, a rarity at the time.
  • Organizational culture and procurement policies made innovation slow and tightly managed.
  • I was embedding automation tools into their existing production line, navigating a constrained and protective ecosystem.

The Solution

  • Designed and deployed a fully automated test and measurement platform optimized for keyless entry modules.
  • Integrated software, electrical test gear, and mechanical fixtures directly into the production line, eliminating manual touch points.
  • Collaborated cross-functionally with mechanical engineers, software developers, and factory management to overcome bureaucratic inertia and drive results under pressure.

The Results

  • Delivered a robust, automated test system plan that enhanced speed and accuracy of testing.
  • The strategy is capable of full deployment within 2 months.
  • Set a new benchmark for test automation capability within the organization.
  • Demonstrated how persistent, integrated engineering work can overcome cultural and structural barriers even in slow-moving industries.
