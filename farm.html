Direct-to-Consumer Marketplace for Fresh Food Farmers

Industry: AgriTech / E-Commerce Project: Direct-to-Consumer Fresh Food Marketplace Role: Consultant / Platform & Operations Advisor

Synopsis

Consulted a US-based startup aiming to connect small-scale fresh food farmers directly with consumers. The goal was to streamline supply chains, increase farmer revenues, and provide consumers with fresher, locally sourced produce.

The Challenge

  • Farmers struggled to reach consumers directly due to fragmented distribution channels.
  • Perishability of fresh produce required efficient logistics and inventory management.
  • Limited market visibility and digital infrastructure for small-scale farmers.

The Solution

  • Designed a digital marketplace platform connecting farmers to consumers.
  • Developed logistics and inventory strategies to minimize waste and optimize delivery.
  • Implemented marketing and customer acquisition strategies to grow consumer adoption.
  • Provided training and onboarding support for farmers to use the platform effectively.

The Results

  • Enabled farmers to sell directly to consumers, increasing their margins.
  • Reduced delivery times and produce spoilage.
  • Achieved initial traction with early adopters, establishing proof of concept.
  • Created scalable framework for expansion to additional regions.
