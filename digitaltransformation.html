Digital Transformation for Fortune 500 Electronics and Digital Company

Digital Technology Digital Transformation Consultant

Synopsis

Part of a consultancy team that led a multi-phase digital transformation to break down silos between global engineering, sales, and customer service teams. The project aimed to accelerate innovation, streamline project delivery, and improve customer satisfaction across multiple countries.

The Challenge

  • Engineering and sales teams operated in silos across regions.
  • Project delivery delays and inefficiencies were common.
  • Limited cross-functional visibility hindered innovation and customer responsiveness.

The Solution

  • Designed and executed a multi-phase transformation program.
  • Conducted global stakeholder workshops to align priorities and processes.
  • Implemented cross-department KPI dashboards for transparency and accountability.
  • Trained teams on agile work practices to improve collaboration and speed.

The Results

  • Reduced project delivery cycle time by 27%.
  • Increased cross-functional collaboration across engineering, sales, and customer service.
  • Improved global customer satisfaction scores.
  • Established repeatable frameworks for ongoing process improvement.
