Pam's Corner

Automated Manufacturing Test System for Carsem and Skyworks

Electronics / Semiconductor Manufacturing Automated Test System Integration Lead Consultant / Project Architect

Synopsis

Led the successful delivery of a multi-million-dollar system integration project for Carsem, supporting Skyworks’ high-speed components used in iPhone manufacturing. The project automated complex testing processes, requiring cross-disciplinary integration of mechanical, hardware, and software systems under demanding on-site conditions.

The Challenge

Skyworks, a leading provider of high-performance components, required Carsem to implement a new automated testing process for products used in iPhones. The project was technically demanding, requiring seamless integration across multiple domains — precision mechanics, advanced hardware, and custom software solutions.

The environment added further complexity. Client policies restricted the consulting team to work only within the production floor. Development, testing, and troubleshooting all had to take place in a noisy, high-pressure setting, with consultants required to remain on-site until daily tasks were completed. The project demanded extended working hours, often standing for 12 hours a day, while ensuring zero disruption to ongoing production lines.

The Solution

As lead consultant, I coordinated efforts across mechanical experts, hardware engineers, and software developers to deliver a fully automated testing system. This required:

  • Designing custom integration strategies that enabled real-time communication between hardware, software, and mechanical systems.
  • Developing robust software solutions in challenging production-floor conditions.
  • Implementing rigorous testing protocols to meet Skyworks’ stringent quality standards for iPhone components.
  • Managing project stakeholders across Carsem and Skyworks to align technical execution with commercial objectives.

The Results

  • After six months of intensive collaboration, the project delivered a reliable, fully automated testing system.
  • The solution improved testing accuracy, reduced cycle times, and met the strict quality standards required by Apple’s supply chain.
  • Despite the challenging work conditions, the project was delivered on time, demonstrating resilience, adaptability, and the ability to integrate complex systems under pressure.
  • The success strengthened Carsem’s role as a key partner for Skyworks in high-performance electronics manufacturing.
