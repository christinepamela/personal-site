What are the stages?
Flat outline — The outer silhouette only. Light pencil, no interior lines.
3D form — Construction lines that give volume: ellipses, grid lines, inner edges.
Shading — Pick one light direction. Hatch the shadow side. Leave highlights blank.
Detail (face only) — Add features after shading: eyelids, nostrils, hair strokes.
Tip: draw on paper, photograph it, then upload here for AI feedback and comparison.
