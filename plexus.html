End-to-End RF & Test Equipment Training for Plexus Workforce

Electronics Manufacturing Corporate Training Trainer / Consultant

Synopsis

To strengthen technical competency across all levels of staff, Plexus Riverside engaged me to design and deliver a structured 3-day training covering RF fundamentals, connector care, and practical usage of test and measurement equipment. The training targeted not only engineers but also technicians, operators, managers, and supply chain staff to ensure a unified understanding of test processes and technology.

The Challenge

  • Plexus needed to upskill a broad, cross-functional team (engineering, production, supply chain) to better support RF and test & measurement activities.
  • Engineers lacked deep technical grounding in RF and advanced analyzers.
  • Technicians and operators required hands-on familiarity with handling equipment and connectors to prevent costly damage.
  • Supply chain staff needed awareness of specifications and product families to make better procurement decisions.

The Solution

I designed a 3-day structured training program with a balance of theory, demos, and hands-on practice. Topics covered included:

  • RF Basics and Connector Care
  • Signal Generators (theory and practice)
  • Spectrum Analyzers (theory and practice)
  • Network Analyzers (including calibration, noise sources, demo kits)
  • Basic Instruments (power meters, counters, function generators, power supplies)

The training also included interactive sessions — quizzes, Q&A, and troubleshooting scenarios — to ensure engagement. Customized sessions were delivered for different audiences (from operator-level connector care to manager-level product overview).

The Results

  • Unified skill upgrade across departments — engineers gained deeper RF knowledge, technicians/operators gained hands-on proficiency, and supply chain staff gained product awareness.
  • Reduced risk of equipment mishandling (e.g., connector care) and calibration errors.
  • Improved Plexus’ test process maturity by aligning technical, operational, and procurement teams.
  • Positive engagement: strong participation across 3 days with practical learning outcomes directly applicable to production and testing.
