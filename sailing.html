Event Operations and Digital Management – Monsoon Cup, Langkawi

Sports / Event Management Operational and Digital Management Consultant / Event Operations Specialist

Synopsis

Consulted on operational and digital management for the Monsoon Cup, a global sailing competition held in Langkawi. The engagement focused on streamlining internal point systems, enhancing media coverage, and managing award solutions to ensure a seamless event experience for participants and spectators.

The Challenge

  • The Monsoon Cup involved multiple international sailing teams, complex point scoring, and real-time event tracking.
  • Media coverage and social engagement were critical to enhancing the event’s global visibility.
  • Challenges included maintaining accuracy in the scoring system, coordinating photography and social media updates, and delivering timely and appropriate awards.

The Solution

  • Designed and managed an internal point tracking system for race results.
  • Oversaw photography coverage and real-time social media updates to engage both on-site and remote audiences.
  • Coordinated award logistics and ensured timely presentation to winners.
  • Integrated digital tools for smooth communication between teams, officials, and media personnel.
  • Technologies Used: Event management & scoring software, photography/media tools, social media platforms for live updates.

The Results

  • Accurate and transparent race scoring system implemented for the event.
  • Improved social media engagement and event visibility across international audiences.
  • Streamlined award presentation process, enhancing participant satisfaction.
  • Overall, contributed to a professional and well-managed global sailing competition.
