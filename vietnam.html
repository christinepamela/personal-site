Satellite Transmission Course – University of Technology, Ho Chi Minh

Education / Satellite Communications Satellite Transmission Course Guest Lecturer

Synopsis

Delivered an outsourced course on satellite transmission, combining both theory and practical applications, to nearly 100 students, lecturers, and department heads.

The Challenge

Students struggled with English as the medium of instruction, which made complex concepts in satellite transmission harder to grasp. The challenge extended to ensuring not only comprehension but also practical application in a technical field where precision is key.

The Solution

  • A translator was engaged to bridge the language gap.
  • Teaching methods were adjusted to simplify and break down complex theories into clear, digestible explanations.
  • Practical demonstrations and examples were emphasized to reinforce understanding.

The Results

  • The course achieved a 70% pass rate despite language barriers and technical complexity.
  • Program was well received by students, faculty, and department heads.
  • Positive feedback on the clarity of delivery and the balance of theory with hands-on practice.
