Overview
I love innovation and creating things. Over the years, I've expanded that passion into strategy, leadership, and transformation work. I have 18+ years of experience driving innovation and business growth with exposure across 30+ countries. My track record includes scaling disruptive business models, leading enterprise engineering and innovation programs, and guiding startups through critical growth phases. My research focuses on decentralized innovation models and how systems evolve in underserved economies. I draw on a strong foundation in emerging technologies and business strategy, and my writing often grows out of scattered observations that eventually take shape as questions I can't shake.
