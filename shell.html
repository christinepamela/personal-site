Offshore Diagnostic System Implementation for Shell Deep-Sea Operations

Oil & Gas / Deep-Sea Operations Offshore Diagnostic System Implementation Consultant / Systems Integrator

Synopsis

Shell needed advanced diagnostic capabilities for their deep-sea drilling equipment to reduce downtime, improve safety, and enable predictive maintenance. We implemented sensor-based monitoring, real-time analytics, and remote diagnostic workflows to enhance equipment reliability and operational efficiency.

The Challenge

  • Deep-sea drilling equipment operates under extreme conditions with high pressure, low temperature, and corrosive seawater.
  • Existing diagnostic systems were fragmented, reactive rather than predictive, and limited engineers’ ability to prevent failures or optimize maintenance.
  • Downtime risked millions in operational costs and posed safety hazards.

The Solution

  • Conducted a comprehensive assessment of existing monitoring and diagnostic systems.
  • Deployed sensor networks and IoT-based monitoring on critical subsea equipment.
  • Built real-time analytics dashboards for onshore and offshore engineers.
  • Conducted workshops and training to upskill teams in interpreting diagnostics and performing predictive maintenance.
  • Integrated the diagnostic system into Shell’s global operational and safety compliance workflows.

The Results

  • Reduced unplanned downtime for offshore drilling equipment.
  • Enabled predictive maintenance, lowering operational costs.
  • Improved safety by early detection of anomalies.
  • Strengthened integration with global engineering operations, enhancing cross-team collaboration and reporting efficiency.
