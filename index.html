New Book Release:
Innovation Underdogs: The Corporate Rebel Playbook

What happens when you try to build breakthrough ideas inside a company that was never designed to change? This book introduces a practical playbook for making innovation stick in complex organizations. It blends case studies, rebel leadership, and the Theta Framework, a visual model that maps six types of innovation across Core, Edge, and Beyond. Instead of breaking the system, Innovation Underdogs shows you how to work through resistance, rally the right team, and scale without losing what works.

Genre: Non-fiction, Business, Innovation
