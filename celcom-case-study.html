The Challenge

Malaysia needs a telco that exports technology, not just imports it.

  • Most innovation today lives in Core operations—better bundles, better networks, better apps
  • Global leaders are shifting investment to Edge (enterprise platforms) and Beyond (moonshots)
  • CelcomDigi has execution excellence but innovation ambition must accelerate
  • Maxis leads the current enterprise battlefield; CelcomDigi can lead the next one

The 70-20-10 Transformation

Gradually rebalance innovation investment from 70/25/5 to 60/30/10 by 2027

Core (60%)

Accelerate fixed-mobile convergence. Maintain the cash flow engine while improving operational efficiency through AI and automation.

Edge (30%)

Scale private 5G and enterprise platforms. This is where global telcos are winning. Build vertical-specific solutions and platform APIs.

Beyond (10%)

Stand up a Moonshot Lab. Focus on three bets: AI-driven networks, digital identity, and satellite connectivity. Move from utility to tech exporter.

Final Takeaway

CelcomDigi has already won scale. Now it must win the future. Malaysia's innovation economy is waiting for a national champion—one that stops importing innovation and starts exporting it.
