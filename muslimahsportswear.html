Globalization Strategy for a Muslimah Sportswear Fashion Brand

Fashion / Sportswear Globalization & Market Expansion Strategy Business Consultant

Synopsis

Advised a Southeast Asian Muslimah sportswear brand on scaling internationally, focusing on entering new markets while maintaining cultural authenticity and brand identity.

The Challenge

  • Brand wanted to move beyond local and regional sales into global markets.
  • Needed to balance cultural identity (modest fashion) with global sportswear trends.
  • Faced complexities in supply chain, pricing, and market positioning across diverse regions.

The Solution

  • Developed a globalization roadmap including target market prioritization.
  • Designed strategies for brand positioning to resonate with both modest fashion and mainstream sportswear audiences.
  • Advised on distribution channels, e-commerce integration, and international retail partnerships.
  • Provided frameworks for pricing, promotions, and supply chain adjustments tailored to different regions.

The Results

  • Clear roadmap for global expansion with phased market entry approach.
  • Strengthened international brand narrative combining modesty, performance, and style.
  • Established groundwork for partnerships with global distributors and online platforms.
