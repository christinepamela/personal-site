Board layout

Motherboard + daughterboard

Main PCB + breakout board. Ideal for wearables — easier to iterate and test.

Single board

Everything on one PCB. Smaller footprint, fewer connectors.

Layers recommended: 2

1 layer

Cheapest to fab. Routing is tight. Good for very simple circuits only.

2 layers

Best for ESP32/Arduino wearables. Power on one side, signals on the other. JLC/JLCPCB-ready.

4 layers

Better signal integrity. Worth it if you need dedicated power/ground planes.

6 layers

RF + high-speed. Overkill unless you're doing antenna design work.

Advanced options for experienced designers ▼
Min 0.2mm for most fabs. 0.3mm is safe and cost-effective.
More = lower impedance + better thermal dissipation in wearables.
Ground plane on bottom reduces EMI from ESP32 WiFi/BLE.
4.7kΩ works for MPU6050 + ESP32 at 3.3V.
100nF placed close to each IC power pin.
